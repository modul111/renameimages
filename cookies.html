Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

✅ Good News: Our core tool does not use tracking cookies. All image processing happens locally in your browser.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them only for essential site functionality and advertising.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Essential: Session cookies to keep the site functional.
  • Advertising (Adsterra): Third-party cookies used to display relevant ads and measure ad performance.

3. How to Control Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect ad display and some site features.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy

4. Third-Party Links

Ads may link to external sites. We are not responsible for their cookie practices. Review their policies separately.
